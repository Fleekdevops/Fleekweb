Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Fleek Tech offer?

Fleek Tech offers a comprehensive range of technology services including web development, mobile app development, cybersecurity, digital marketing, cloud hosting, AI integration, business consultancy, graphic design, and specialized solutions like Zglobal-Connect for ISPs.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, medium projects 1-3 months, and complex enterprise solutions 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing: fixed-price projects, hourly rates (KES 2,500-5,000/hour), and monthly retainers. Use our Solutions Finder or Consultation Calculator for accurate estimates tailored to your needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support plans starting from KES 5,000/month covering maintenance, updates, security monitoring, and 24/7 technical assistance.

What is your development process?

Our 5-step process includes: 1) Discovery & Planning, 2) Design & Prototyping, 3) Development, 4) Testing & Quality Assurance, 5) Deployment & Maintenance.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we serve clients globally with remote collaboration tools and flexible time zone arrangements. We accept payments in multiple currencies.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, PHP, WordPress, Flutter, React Native, AWS, Google Cloud, and various AI/ML frameworks.

How do I get started?

Simply schedule a free consultation using our contact form, use the Solutions Finder, or contact us directly at +254 758 175 057.

Service Level Agreement (SLA)

For Support & Maintenance Services

1. Service Availability

  • Basic Plan: 99% uptime guarantee
  • Pro Plan: 99.5% uptime guarantee
  • Enterprise Plan: 99.9% uptime guarantee

2. Response Times

  • Critical Issues: 1 hour response, 4 hour resolution
  • High Priority: 2 hour response, 8 hour resolution
  • Medium Priority: 4 hour response, 24 hour resolution
  • Low Priority: 8 hour response, 48 hour resolution

3. Maintenance Windows

Scheduled maintenance occurs Sundays 2:00 AM - 6:00 AM EAT with 48-hour advance notice.

4. Service Credits

If uptime falls below guarantee, clients receive service credits as follows:

  • 98-99%: 10% monthly credit
  • 95-98%: 25% monthly credit
  • Below 95%: 50% monthly credit

5. Exclusions

SLA excludes downtime due to: client-side issues, third-party services, force majeure events, scheduled maintenance.
